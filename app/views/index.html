Single User View

Internal facing single user view that displays multiple PCN's for single users. This should help identify repeat offenders or recurring issues with vulnerable people building up many large penalties.

Prototypes

  • Internal PCN search, customer has mutliple PCN's matched

Scenarios for testing

  • Customer has received their first ever PCN (simple case)
  • Customer has received a PCN, they have a number of full matches based on NHS no. or on exact contact details
  • Customer has received a PCN, they have a number of partial matches
  • Customer has received a PCN, they have a number of full matches and partial matches (complex case)
