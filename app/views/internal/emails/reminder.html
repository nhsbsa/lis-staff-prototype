How to avoid penalties in the future

  • Make sure that your GP surgery, dental practice, benefits organisations and the NHS Business Services Authority have your correct name and home address, so that your details match.
  • If you have an NHS exemption certificate (medical or maternity), make sure it does not expire.
  • If you are entitled to claim free prescriptions make sure that you tick the correct box on the back on the prescription.
  • Make sure that you tick the correct box on the dental patient declaration form when claiming free or reduced cost NHS dental services.

NHS Penalty Charge Notices are issued in accordance with the NHS (Penalty Charge) Regulations 1999. The NHS Business Services Authority (NHSBSA) works on behalf of the NHS to check claims for free prescriptions and recover charges from patients who incorrectly claim they do not have to pay. This is done to protect public funds and provide best value for money for the taxpayer.
