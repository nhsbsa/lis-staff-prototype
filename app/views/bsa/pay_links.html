Pay a Penalty Charge Notice

You can pay off your Penalty Charge Notice in full so that the debt is cleared straight away or you can pay it off in instalments.

You will need a debit or credit card to make the payment.

You will also need your 9-digit PCN number. This can be found on your Penalty Charge Notice letter.

Which type of NHS Penalty Charge Notice are you paying?

  • Prescriptions – pay now
  • Dental treatment – pay now

If your PCN number begins with ‘BECS/DR’ you need to make your payment to NHS Dental Services here.

Don't delay

It is important to start paying your Penalty Charge Notice as soon as possible. Delaying payment could lead to an extra fine of up to £50. Check your Penalty Charge Notice letter for your payment deadline.
