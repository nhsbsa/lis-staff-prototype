Pay or challenge a Penalty Charge Notice

If you have received an NHS Penalty Charge Notice you must respond as soon as possible.

You have 3 options:

  • pay your Penalty Charge Notice – in full or in instalments
  • tell us if there has been a mistake – answer a few questions so we can better match your details
  • tell us if you have an exceptional reason not to pay

You will need your 9-digit PCN number. This can be found on your Penalty Charge Notice letter.
